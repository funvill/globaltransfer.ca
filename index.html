Global Transfer Services is the exclusive contractor for Body Removal for the BC Coroners Service as well as for several funeral homes in the Lower Mainland. We have been in business since 2001 and have completed in excess of 35,000 removals.

At Global Transfer Services, we always provide professional, dignified removals of the deceased and treat each case we’re given with the respect we all deserve. With 15 years’ experience in the body removal business we have earned an excellent reputation in the industry and are always seeking ways to improve ourselves to better serve our clients by welcoming input from our staff and clients. Staff are trained in dealing with the bereaved and we have good relationships with hospitals, police and the funeral industry. Global Transfer is not just a removal service—it is a business dedicated to doing it right for the deceased.
